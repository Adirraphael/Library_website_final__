External Resources

Here are some valuable tools and platforms to support your research and studies:

  • Google Scholar - Search for academic papers, theses, and articles.
  • JSTOR - Access academic journals, books, and primary sources.
  • Purdue OWL - Writing and citation resources for students.
  • Project Gutenberg - Free access to thousands of eBooks.
  • W3Schools - Tutorials for web development and coding.
